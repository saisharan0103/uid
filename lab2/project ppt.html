			                                               Setting SMART Project Goals
                      SMART is an acronym that stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant and Time-Bound.  

                        SMART goals are a staple for planning and executing successful projects. It takes a deeper
                                       look into the finer details your audience care about, such as:

                                                          Project plan and schedule,
                                                                   Resources,
                                                               Project timelines,
                                                                   Milestones, 
                                                         Potential roadblocks and more
